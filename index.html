Take back your day.

With Docbot you can say goodbye to spending your day at the doctor's office for minor medical issues.

Docbot is an artifically intellegent doctor. Built by medical professionals.

Simply text Docbot your symptoms, answer a few clinical questions, and Docbot will provide you with a diagnose. Docbot can determine whether over-the-counter medications are appropriate for your treatment or if necessary, Docbot can connect you to a board certified physician.

We are on a mission to open up healthcare access globally.

We believe in healthcare equality for humanity. No matter where you are in the world or what language you speak, we are committed to building technologies that will provide you with instant access great medical care.
